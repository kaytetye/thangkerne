curlew

Burhinus grallarius

Ilerepe thangkerne arwengerrpe apenye. Repe aynenke perrkaltye, ayepayte kape wantakerrertetye. Ilere nyartepe thangkerne errpatye.

The curlew looks a bit like the bush turkey. It eats grasshoppers, grubs and other insects. This bird brings bad luck.
