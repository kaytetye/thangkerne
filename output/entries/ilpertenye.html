Wedge-tailed eagle

Aquila audax

Ilpertenyepe thangkerne errpwerle alkenhe intetyeng-intetye arltere kape aherne apenye. Thangkerne nyartepe weye aynewene. Ilpertenyelepe weye kwereyenge alarrenke aherre, arinenge, kaperle, arlewatyerre kape thangkerne kelye-kelye.

The wedge-tailed eagle is a big black bird with little spots of white and brown. The eagle is a meat-eater which hunts and kills on its own. It eats kangaroos, euro, lizards, goannas and other small birds.
